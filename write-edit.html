Write & Edit

Communications / Write & Edit

I work as a freelance writer and editor.

I have extensive experience as a writer and as an editor. I have written for a diverse range of audiences across multiple topics. I am able to write, edit and finalise documents. I have written and published in academic journals, as a news journalist, as a government researcher, for market research and for legal documents. I have worked as a ghost writer for various clients, editing business, legal and academic documents.

I also offer a specialised course on ‘Writing Well in English’. This is a 12-week course designed for 2nd language English speakers working in communications and marketing roles, wishing to improve their written English and document preparation. This course covers business emails as well as marketing, writing and editing for more effective English business practice.
