Armstrong Ideas Work

Welcome! It has taken me a long time to be here.

I offer a range of services, broadly I focus on supporting people. I do this in various ways; either as a a coach (with a focus on the emotional and the personal aspects to performance [link to coaching]), or as a trainer (with a focus on the cognitive, knowledge based aspects of learning and excelling [link to training]), or as a communications specialist (with a focus on writing, editing and presenting information, or empowering you to do those things [link to communications]).

What I Offer

Coach

As a coach I offer a space, for thinking and for feeling. Using the Thinking Environment, I listen, deeply. In this space I enable clients to make empowered decisions, to reflect on what works, what they wish to keep and what they wish to discard.
